Payout in 2 steps

Step-1: Generate and Initiate Payment In this step we have to Generate the payout report. It can be scheduled automatically. The system will generate the report for a pre-defined interval. In manual process, we have to click on the below "Generate Payout Report" Button. After generating report, we can initiate the payment. Click "Process Payment" button after selecting record(s)to initiate a payment. The payout record will be listed in Ready For Payment.
Step-2: Download the report and Mark as paid In Ready For Payment page we have to Download the payout report and make the payment. After making payment, please change the status to Mark as Paid by clicking "Mark as Paid" button after selecting record(s). After this step, it will be considered as payment processes complete. All the steps are Mandatory
